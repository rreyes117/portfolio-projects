## Featured Projects

### 1. From 40-Yard Dash to Draft Day: Evaluating NFL Draft Prospects Using Numerical Analysis  
**College | Duke University | Numerical Analysis**

A data-driven framework for evaluating NFL draft prospects by combining NFL Combine metrics with college performance data. Rather than predicting raw future statistics, this project constructs a continuous *prospect score* that captures readiness and upside.

**Key methods:**
- Least squares and ridge regression  
- Principal Component Analysis (PCA)  
- Support Vector Regression (SVR)  
- Monte Carlo simulation for robustness and uncertainty  
- Model comparison across Combine-only, college-only, and combined feature sets  

**Outcome:**  
The combined models significantly outperform single-source approaches, highlighting the importance of contextual performance alongside raw athletic traits.

### 3. Analysis of Climate Change and Flood Risk  
**High School | Modeling the Future Challenge (Top 10 National Finish)**

A team-based actuarial and data science project analyzing flood risk for North Carolina and Missouri counties. The project integrates climate data, geographic risk factors, and insurance claims data to model flood probability and expected insurance losses.

**Key methods:**
- Logistic regression for flood probability estimation  
- K-nearest-neighbors regression for expected claim severity  
- Feature engineering using lagged climate variables  
- Risk stratification and policy recommendations  

**Outcome:**  
The project placed **Top 10 nationally** out of 150+ teams and was recognized for its combination of statistical rigor and practical policy insight.
